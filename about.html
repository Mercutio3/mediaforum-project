About Us

Welcome

The Media Review Forum allows users to upload reviews of different media they've consumed. In contrast to existing forums, which tend to specialize in a single kind of media (e.g. books, films, TV shows, etc.), this forum serves as a general hub for reviews on a wider variety of media. This forum can be used by casual fans, media enthusiasts, and critics alike. We bring everyone together with love for media.


This forum was developed as part of a Web App Development course at Constructor University.

The GitHub repository containing this project's files can be found here.


Features

  • Review Submission: Submit reviews with a variety of attributes about a variety of media.
  • Account Creation: Dedicated profile pages for each user.
  • Community Interaction: Interact with different users simulaneously via likes, comments, and ratings on reviews and profiles.
  • Review Browsing: Browse and filter reviews according to desired search parameters.
  • Cosmetic Customization: Different color themes to best suit your taste.

Frequently Asked Questions

What can I do without an account?

You are free to browse and search through all of our reviews, as well as see who has posted them and commented on them.

Why should I create an account?

Creating an account gives you access to all of this website's features. This includes the ability to post your own reviews and customize your profile.
